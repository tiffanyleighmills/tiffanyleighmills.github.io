Tiffany Leigh Mills

Tiffany Mills born - 04/03/1992 - is a dedicated actress and model based out of tampa florida.

She started tattoo modeling in 2016 and has been featured in numerous international magazines. She began acting in 2018 and has done commercials, short independent films and has the lead female role in a handful of films coming out 2019. Tiffany is versatile in the roles she takes and can memorize scripts quickly. She has dramatic arts training. She can sing. She loves rom-coms and horror genres but excels in any genre. She is an advocate for domestic violence and mental health and is very much a people person who is always smiling and happy. She is represented by an agency in Florida.
